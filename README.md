# DivyaAbility Foundation

DivyaAbility Foundation is a registered non-profit organization in India focused on helping people with disabilities build a better and independent future.

We create accessible technology, provide education support, and develop real-world systems that solve practical problems.

---

## Our Mission

To provide exclusive education, job training, and support systems that help people with disabilities become independent and confident.

---

## What We Do

- Scribe support for visually impaired students  
- Accessible learning platforms  
- computer and job training  
- Scholarships and support  

---

## Technology

- Learning Management System (LMS)  
- Scribe booking platform  
- Web applications for accessibility
